## Verified Claims (Tier A/B only)

1. Greene's own, specific, on-the-record technique for the Jefferson squat (A1) — the clearest piece of verified programming detail available for this legend: wide stance, bar between the legs, smaller plates for depth, glutes/knees cue, paired with the hip-adductor machine.
2. Greene's own stated rep-range preference for legs, from a Muscle & Fitness feature quoting him directly (B1): roughly 15-20 reps taken to failure, described as where he personally feels the best combination of power, strength, and mind-muscle connection.
3. Existing philosophy quotes (A3, A4) remain valid and are kept.
4. No official program, DVD, or branded system independent of the above publishes a verified sets/reps table for a full session or week.

## Gaps — documented honestly

- **No verified day-by-day split.** Every 5-day-split table in circulation (C1) is an uncited, mutually-copied aggregator template.
- **No verified warm-up protocol, working-set count, or intensity technique beyond "working to failure."**
- **The Dubai leg-workout video with Andrew Jacked (C2) was not transcribed** — the best remaining lead for a real, dated, on-camera Greene training session, since it is hosted on his own official YouTube channel.
- Greene has published extensive philosophy/mindset content but very little session-level programming detail in his own words that survives scrutiny — this entry should stay honest about that imbalance rather than borrow a Tier C table to fill the gap.
