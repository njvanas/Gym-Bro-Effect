## Verified Claims (Tier A confirmed existence; Tier C corroborated shape)

1. **Columbu is a genuine, prolific self-author** — at least six training/nutrition books under his own name across 1977-1985, which is more self-authored material than almost any other name in this batch (A1, A2).
2. **The "14-day, 6-days-on/1-off, twice-per-bodypart, AM/PM split" shape is consistently reported across four independent secondary sources** (C1-C4) — this consistency is a good sign the underlying claim traces to something real (likely his own books), even though none of the four sources gives a direct page citation.
3. **A powerlifting-influenced approach is corroborated across sources**: heavy compound lifts (bench, squat, deadlift) including heavy singles/doubles/triples, consistent with his known Olympic weightlifting/powerlifting background before bodybuilding (C1).

## Gaps

- **No source in this pass directly quotes a page or chapter of *Winning Bodybuilding* or any other Columbu book with a specific citation.** The consistent 14-day-split claim across C1-C4 is a reasonably strong circumstantial signal that it traces to his books, but this file cannot certify it as a verbatim quote.
- The specific numeric tables (C3's descending-rep schemes) appear only in one source (Fitness Volt) and are not corroborated by the other three — treat those exact numbers as the single-source, lower-confidence tier within Tier C.
