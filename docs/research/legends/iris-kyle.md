## Verified Claims (Tier B, cross-corroborated)

1. **An explicit, repeatedly-stated philosophy of training "by the basics," not by rigid programming** — directly quoted in both 2009 (B2) and 2012 (B1), years apart, which is strong corroboration of a genuine, consistent personal philosophy rather than a one-off soundbite.
2. **A specific, real back-training routine with sets/reps was published under her name** in a *Flex*/M&F feature (B2) — this is a genuinely citable Tier B program, not a Tier C reconstruction.
3. **Off-season training frequency is directly quoted in two separate years but with some variance**: "5 days/week, 2 off" (2012, B1) vs. "4 days/week, 2-on/1-off/2-on" (2008, B3) — both are Tier B direct quotes, so the honest move is to state both and flag that her self-reported schedule varied across career stages, not to average them into one invented number.
4. **Contest-prep frequency is consistently reported as daily training**, including 2 bodyparts/day and daily cardio, across both 2008 and 2012 quotes (B1, B3).
5. **A specific, quoted exercise-sequencing rule**: always opens back day with pull-ups as a warm-up, then sequences remaining exercises by feel rather than a fixed order (B2).
