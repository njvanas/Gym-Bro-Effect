## Verified Claims (Tier A confirmed)

1. **Bill Pearl authored multiple, genuinely self-published, best-selling training books** spanning at least 1960s–2010s reissues (*Keys to the Inner Universe*, *Getting Stronger*, *Beyond the Universe*) — this is the strongest kind of Tier A material available for any legend in this roster: an actual authored text, not a reconstructed interview.
2. **General rep-range guidance attributed to his books**: 8-12 reps for hypertrophy, 15-30 reps for abs/calves or endurance work, with routines rotated every 6-8 weeks to avoid plateaus (per C1's synthesis of the books — this specific framing should be verified against the book text directly before treating as a direct quote, but it's consistent across secondary summaries).
3. **A specific historical detail is corroborated**: his 1950s training was guided by trainer Leo Stern, and Pearl documented that program himself in his later books (C1).

## Gaps

- **This research pass did not obtain and directly quote the primary book text** — all specific sets/reps tables (C1, C2) are third-party reconstructions attributed to the books, not verified page citations. A future pass with direct access to *Keys to the Inner Universe* or *Getting Stronger* could upgrade this from "well-attributed reconstruction" to fully verified Tier A quotes.
- No single, canonical "Bill Pearl program" exists — his own books contain many different programs for many different goals (sport-specific, beginner-to-advanced, bulk, cutting), so any `styles.json` entry should state which specific program/chapter it's drawing from rather than implying one universal Bill Pearl routine.
